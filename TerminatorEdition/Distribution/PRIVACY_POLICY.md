# Privacy Policy - Craig-O Clean Terminator Edition

**Effective Date**: January 24, 2026
**Last Updated**: January 24, 2026

## Introduction

NeuralQuantum.ai LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how Craig-O Clean Terminator Edition ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information (Optional)**
- Full name (if you create an account)
- Email address (if you sign up with email)
- Apple ID information (if you use Sign in with Apple)
- Profile avatar (optional, stored locally and in your iCloud)

**Settings & Preferences**
- App settings and preferences
- Autonomous mode configurations
- Thresholds for memory and disk usage
- Ollama AI preferences (if enabled)

### Information Collected Automatically

**System Metrics (Local Only)**
- CPU usage
- Memory usage
- Disk space usage
- Running processes information
- Browser tab counts
- System health scores

**App Usage Data**
- Features used
- Settings changes
- Cleanup operation results

**IMPORTANT**: All system metrics and app usage data are processed **locally on your device only**. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
All system monitoring and analysis happens entirely on your Mac. We do not send any system metrics, process information, or usage data to external servers.

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your settings and preferences are synced across your devices
- Your profile information (name, email, avatar) is stored in your iCloud
- All iCloud data uses Apple's secure infrastructure
- We do not have access to your iCloud data

### AI Processing (Optional)
If you enable Ollama AI features:
- All AI processing happens locally on your Mac
- No data is sent to external AI services
- Ollama models run entirely offline
- We do not collect or analyze AI interactions

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All app data is stored locally on your Mac in:
  - `~/Library/Application Support/CraigOTerminator/`
  - macOS UserDefaults
- This includes:
  - Your settings
  - App preferences
  - Process history
  - Cleanup logs

### iCloud Storage (Optional)
If enabled, the following is stored in your iCloud:
- User profile (name, email)
- Profile avatar (compressed, max 64KB)
- App settings and preferences
- This data is encrypted by Apple and we cannot access it

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.**

### Third-Party Services

**Apple Services**
- Sign in with Apple (optional)
- iCloud sync (optional)
- TestFlight (for beta testing)
- Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

**Ollama (Optional)**
- Open-source AI platform
- Runs entirely on your Mac
- No data sent to external servers
- Subject to Ollama's terms (local only)

### Service Providers
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Data Security

### Security Measures
- All sensitive operations require user authentication
- Admin privileges requested only when necessary
- System-critical processes protected from unauthorized access
- All data encrypted at rest (macOS FileVault)
- iCloud data encrypted in transit and at rest (Apple's encryption)

### Account Security
- Sign in with Apple provides enhanced security
- Email/password accounts use secure hashing
- Profile data encrypted before iCloud sync

## Your Rights & Choices

### Access Your Data
You can access all your data through the app's settings:
- View profile information
- Export settings
- Review cleanup history

### Delete Your Data

**Local Data**
- Settings → Advanced → Reset All Settings
- Or manually delete: `~/Library/Application Support/CraigOTerminator/`

**iCloud Data**
- Settings → Account → Sign Out
- This removes all synced data from iCloud

**Account Deletion**
- Settings → Account → Sign Out → Delete Account
- Permanently removes all local and cloud data

### Disable Features
You can disable:
- iCloud sync
- Apple Sign In
- Autonomous mode
- Ollama AI features
- Notifications

## Children's Privacy

Craig-O Clean is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## International Users

The App is designed primarily for users in the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located.
